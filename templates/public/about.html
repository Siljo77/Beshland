Mateja Sijan is a young artist, often misunderstood because she is ahead of her time in many ways. All her life she wanted to work in some form of art and she found it in Beshland. Now her mission is to help others find that same answer and find a place where they can relax and have fun while expressing themselves artistically. Mateja has traveled and lived in many countries and thus broadened her horizons about life, which also allowed her to grow as an artist as well as an inexhaustible source of inspiration.

BESHLAND is a place where people come to have fun and relax and maybe find an artist in themselves. BESHLAND is a world unto itself for all people with the feeling that they want to build something with their own hands while having fun with their friends. BESHLAND is actually a place that is an extension of Mateja, as much as it is her world she has decided to share her work and her knowledge with others.
